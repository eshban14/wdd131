Restaurant Website Plan

Site Name

Name: Raees Food

Description: The name "Raees Food" reflects a high-end dining experience where customers can enjoy best meals in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. It emphasizes quality and exclusivity.

Optional Domain: Raeesfood.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as the primary online presence for "Raees Food," showcasing its menu, services, and reservation options. It will provide customers with an easy way to explore the restaurant's offerings, make reservations, and learn about its story and values.

Scenarios

  • What are the restaurant's signature dishes?
  • How can I make a reservation for a special event?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #d32f2f (Red) - Used for headings and accents.

Secondary Color: #388e3c (Green) - Used for links and buttons.

Background Color: #f4f1ea (Light Beige) - Used for the background.

Text Color: #3e2723 (Dark Brown) - Used for body text.

Typography

Headings: Helvetica, sans-serif - Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3).

Body Text: Georgia, serif - Used for paragraphs and general text.

Wireframes

Mobile View: A single-column layout with a hero banner at the top, followed by sections for About Us, Menu, Services, and Contact. The navigation menu will be collapsible.

Mobile Wireframe Sketch Placeholder

Desktop View: A multi-column layout with a fixed navigation bar at the top. The hero banner will span the full width, and the content will be organized into a grid for the menu and services sections.
